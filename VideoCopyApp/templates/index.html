FPS & Length Agnostic

Multi-View Aware

Threshold-based Hamming similarity on perceptual hashes catches duplicates filmed from different angles.

How it works

Four steps to detection

01 —

Upload Video

User uploads a video. The system temporarily stores it for processing.

02 —

Perceptual Fingerprint

20 frames sampled by percentage. Each resized to 16×16, hashed by pixel-vs-mean comparison.

03 —

FHE Comparison

Signatures encrypted with Paillier. Homomorphic subtraction computed on ciphertext — no plaintext leak.

04 —

Verdict & Storage

Unique videos stored in cloud. Duplicates logged with a reference to the original — never double-stored.
